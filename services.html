Free Services at RNR Tire Express

Every tire and wheel purchase at RNR Tire Express comes with our Premium Customer Care Package—a suite of free services designed to enhance your investment and keep your ride safe and smooth. From nitrogen inflation to roadside assistance, we’ve got you covered at no extra cost.

What’s Included?

  • Nitrogen Inflation: Enjoy a safer, longer-lasting ride with free nitrogen inflation. Unlike oxygen, nitrogen maintains consistent pressure, reduces blowout risks, saves on gas, and prevents rim rust by eliminating condensation.
  • Flat Tire Repair: Get free repairs on tread punctures up to 1/4″, no matter where you bought the tire. Our technicians inspect for hidden damage to ensure your safety.
  • Alignment Checks: Keep your tires in top shape with free alignment checks. Proper alignment extends tire life, improves handling, and enhances road safety.
  • Tire Rotation & Balance: Prolong tire life and eliminate wear with free rotation and balancing. We’ll inspect tread, inflation, and lug nuts while minimizing vehicle vibration.
  • Professional Installation: Our expert team provides free, top-notch installation with every purchase, ensuring your tires and wheels are road-ready.
  • 12-Month Roadside Assistance: Drive with peace of mind thanks to our 24/7 Road Warrior Assistance program, included for a year. Call (866) 353-1351 for towing, jump starts, lock-out help, or fuel delivery.

Why It Matters

These complimentary services don’t just add value—they contribute to the longevity and performance of your tires and wheels. With Shane and the RNR team, you’re not just buying tires; you’re getting a complete care package to keep you rolling worry-free.
