Flexible Financing at RNR Tire Express

Getting the tires and wheels you want has never been easier with RNR Tire Express’s Easy Payment Options. Our "Everyone's Approved" plans mean no credit checks—just affordable, flexible financing tailored to your budget. Drive away today and pay later!

Why Choose RNR Financing?

At RNR, we believe everyone deserves their dream ride, regardless of credit history. Here’s what makes our payment plans stand out:

  • No Credit Needed: With our "Everyone’s Approved" program, we skip the credit checks and work with you to find a plan that fits.
  • Custom Payment Schedules: Choose weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly payments to match your budget.
  • 6 Months Same As Cash: Pay off your tires and wheels within 6 months (or 120 days at some locations) and enjoy the regular cash price—no interest!
  • Low Down Payment: Start with as little as $20 down and six months interest-free at ANR Express.
  • Penalty-Free Cancellation: Change your mind? Cancel anytime with no penalties.

How to Get Started

Ready to roll? Bring these to your local RNR Tire Express: your driver’s license, pay stub (proof of income), vehicle registration, and proof of residence (e.g., a utility bill). We’ll set you up with a payment plan that works for you.

Payment Made Simple

Pay online (at participating stores), over the phone, or in-store with cash, credit card, or money order. There’s no need to pay upfront—get your tires and wheels now and manage payments later.

Service Area Note: Availability varies by location. Contact your nearest RNR store to confirm options in your area.
